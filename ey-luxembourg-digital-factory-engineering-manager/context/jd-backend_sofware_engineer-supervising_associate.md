**Build a Better Working World with EY Digital Factory**

At EY, we’re committed to shaping the future with confidence. As part of our Digital Factory team, you’ll work at the intersection of technology and innovation, helping to deliver cutting-edge solutions that transform businesses globally.

This is your opportunity to take on a leadership-oriented technical role, guiding backend engineering practices while remaining hands-on in building secure, scalable, and reliable services that power EY’s digital platforms.

---

**The Opportunity**

As a **Supervising Associate Backend Engineer**, you will:

- Lead the design and development of APIs, data access layers, and integrations.
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical decision-making.
- Collaborate with Product, Architecture, Frontend, and Data teams to deliver high-quality solutions.
- Drive best practices in coding, security, and observability across the team.

---

**What We’re Looking For**

- **Agility and Growth Mindset:** Ability to adapt and thrive in a fast-paced environment.
- **Technical Leadership:** Experience guiding teams and influencing architecture decisions.
- **Collaboration and Inclusivity:** Building trust and fostering diverse perspectives.

---

**Qualifications**

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.
- **7+ years of professional experience** in backend development, with at least **1 year in a leadership or mentoring capacity**.
- Expertise in building backend services and APIs using **.NET/C# or Python**.
- Strong knowledge of relational and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, SQL Server, MongoDB).
- Hands-on experience with **CI/CD pipelines**, automated testing, and code reviews.
- Working knowledge of **Azure Cloud**, containerization (Docker), and orchestration (Kubernetes).
- Familiarity with messaging and event streaming (Kafka, RabbitMQ, Azure Service Bus).
- Solid understanding of **security fundamentals** and observability practices.

---

**Preferred Skills**

- Experience with **microservices**, DDD, CQRS, and event-driven architectures.
- Exposure to GraphQL, gRPC, API gateways.
- Performance optimization and caching strategies (Redis).
- Background in regulated environments or financial services.
- Familiarity with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform/Bicep).
- Proven ability to lead distributed teams and drive technical excellence.

---

**Key Responsibilities**

- **Technical Leadership:** Guide design reviews, mentor team members, and enforce coding standards.
- **Service & API Development:** Architect and implement robust APIs and backend services.
- **Quality & Reliability:** Ensure high test coverage, implement CI/CD, and maintain observability.
- **Security & Compliance:** Apply secure coding practices and support audits.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Drive automation, reduce operational toil, and improve developer experience.

- Collaborate with global teams in a diverse and inclusive environment.
- Access continuous investment in your well-being and career development.
- Shape the future of digital innovation at EY.
